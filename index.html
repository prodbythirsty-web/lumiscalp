How It Works

Light that reaches where it counts

Red Light

Absorbed directly by follicle cells, red light supports cellular energy production — giving follicles the fuel they need to function at their best. Reaches 2–3mm into the scalp.

Near-Infrared

Penetrates deeper (4–5mm) to support circulation around the follicle root and help calm scalp inflammation that can interfere with the hair growth cycle.
